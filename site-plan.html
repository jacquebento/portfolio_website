Overview

Purpose

If you want an application or a website do you look for a software developer or a web designer? How about both? I am a computer programmer with a graphic design background. These skills will be at your service to build a product functional and visually interesting. Do you want to know more? I am a Salesforce Admin Certified, bringing productivity and design to your Salesforce environment. Here you will find my projects and accomplishments to give you an idea of the kind of service I can provide.

Audience

The audience of my website are people interested in knowing my work. These people might be future clients, employers interested in information about me and my work for an eventual hiring and also to satisfy the curiosity of friends and family about what I do professionally. On this site they will find samples of projects done by me, they will also know my level of knowledge in the various tools related to the services I provide.
